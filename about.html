About Pine City Mall

WHO WE ARE

An impressive 48 632mÂ² The Pine City Mall is located on Old Pretoria Road in the fast-growing, cosmopolitan Midrand central business district and is the only mall of its kind in the region. The centre was built around massive, awe-inspiring granite boulders, which are a great conversation piece and perfect meeting point for shoppers.

A welcome and trusted shopping destination since 1992, Pine City Mall offers a convenient mix of major retailers like Pick n Pay and Game, fashion stores like Truworths and Mr Price, a health gym and several fast food outlets.

The mall also has specialist offices housing health services such as dentists. Pine City Mall is positioned as a safe, customer-friendly mall with 24-hour on-site security and offers a relaxed, interesting and pleasant shopping experience.
